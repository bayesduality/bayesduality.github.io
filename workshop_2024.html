Bayes-Duality Workshop 2024

About Program Participants 2023 Workshop

Program

The tentative program is here

The Bayes-duality project workshop 2024 is the second edition of an ongoing effort to socialize the project ideas of “AI that learns adaptively, robustly, and continously, like humans” through Bayesian principles. The main goal of the meeting is to discuss the long-term goals of the project, set some short-term ones, and foster collaborations among the attendees.

Invited speakers will give talks and tutorials about related topics, and encouraged to participate in discussion sessions with members to understand their work, advise them and spark new collaborations. Project members will also have opportunities to present recent work, published and on-going. Overall, we hope that as with the previous edition, the proposed activities help attendees increase their research's impact, outreach and help them network.

Weeks 1 and 2 focus on talks and tutorials by invited speakers related to the big-picture goals of the overall project. Week 3 will focus on talks about individual projects, with hands-on activities to foster possible collaborations, and start new projects that we hope continue beyond the workshop.

This workshop is partially supported by the Bayes-Duality Project, JST CREST Grant Number JPMJCR2112: “A new Bayes-duality principle for adaptive, robust, and lifelong learning of AI systems”.
